ABOUT ME

Who I am?

I am Sola, a seasoned counselor whose personal journey from a shy, self-doubting young woman to a resilient and empowered individual has shaped my approach to helping others navigate life's challenges.

My early experiences in young love and the subsequent heartbreak profoundly instilled in me the significance of a healthy self-esteem, and how to manage abandonment and rejection. These experiences, coupled with my own struggles in marriage and encounters with financial distress, allowed me to develop a deep empathy and compassion for those who seek guidance.
